Preserving Malaysia's Cultural Heritage

Welcome

Traditional games are heritage-based recreational activities passed down from previous generations. They typically involve simple tools, rules, and group participation.

These games promote physical coordination, mental agility, and cultural values, making them an important aspect of Malaysia's intangible cultural heritage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I become a member?

A: Simply fill in the registration form and we will contact you via email within 2 business days to complete your membership.

Q: Is membership free?

A: Yes! Membership is completely free for all visitors. We believe in making cultural heritage accessible to everyone.

Q: What age groups can join?

A: All age groups are welcome! We have special programs for children, adults, and senior citizens.

Q: Can I access resources online?

A: Yes, members get access to our digital portal with e-books, videos, and interactive learning materials.
